I set our lesson fees in the appropriate currency based on the language of instruction and the student’s country of residence. For lessons conducted in **French or English**, which are available to students residing overseas, the fees are determined according to international standard pricing, as outlined below: 【Fees】 French / English Lessons (Euro Area): 45 € French / English Lessons (USD Regions): 45 USD Japanese Lessons (Japan): 4,500 JPY Payment will be processed in the currency corresponding to your country of residence. This pricing structure has been established to reflect regional currency values and international market rates, ensuring fair and transparent conditions for all students. We kindly ask for your understanding and appreciation.
